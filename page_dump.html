Patek Philippe 5119G-001 description

Introduced in 2006, the 5119G-001 is a perfect example of the Calatrava in its most recognizable form - with the hobnail bezel as conceived in the eighties. The case on this one measures 36mm across. The case is white gold, as indicated by the -G in the reference number. While the 5116 sports an enamel dial, the dial on the 5119 is lacquered.
